Elephants are the largest land animals and are mostly found in Asia and Africa. The Asian elephants are a bit smaller and have shorter ears than the African ones. These gentle giants are known for their peaceful nature and close family bonds. They use their long trunks to drink water, eat food, and even show love to one another. An IQ test would show that elephants are among the most intelligent animals on Earth. They can remember people, places, and sounds for many years.

Even though elephants look huge and powerful, they are kind and emotional creatures. They love to play in rivers and cover themselves with mud to keep their skin cool.
